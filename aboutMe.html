Who is this guy?

Prior to Web Development, I studied and worked in the biological sciences. I attended University of California, San Diego, and graduated with a Masters in Biology and a Bachelors in Physiology/Neuroscience. Upon graduating, I started working as a Study Coordinator at Crown Bioscience and worked my way up to a Study Director. Although I have enjoyed my journey thus far and have no regrets, I know that deep down my passion lies within the software industry. I am not only passionate about learning different coding languages and logic, but am also very intrigued by the intricacies of how computers work. I love how powerful and useful computers are, and how they will continue to improve and serve us from decades onward.
